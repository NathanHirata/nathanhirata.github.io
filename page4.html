Ball Clicker Game

This is my online multiplayer game I made for app academy. How to play: Type your name at the top, then use your mouse to click the ball, whoever clicks the ball first wins! The game title "Klicke auf Ball" is in German and translates to "Click the Ball". I chose to make this game because I wanted to make a sort of unique game that more than 10 people can play at the same time. The hardest part is getting the game to run smoothly with lots of people playing. I learned how to host a game on a server and invite people to my website online.
